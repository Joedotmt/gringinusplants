Gringinu's Plants - Plant Care Tips

Nurturing Your Green Companions

Lighting Matters

Match plants with their light needs. Consider natural sunlight and duration of exposure.

Water Wisely

Avoid overwatering; let topsoil dry between waterings.

Nutrient Boost

Fertilize during the growing season with a balanced, water-soluble fertilizer.
